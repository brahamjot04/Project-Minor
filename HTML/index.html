BAKEOLOGY is your go-to bakery and special occasion sweets shop, and is a place where our team carries out goatland's tradition of showcasing the love of fun flavor combinations. From the harmonious union between a mixes of cereals flours and legumes flours, a new line of tasty specialitiesrich in fiber and vegetable proteins in born.
